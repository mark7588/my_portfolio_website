Who Am I

With three years of experience as a Data Engineer at leading financial institutions - Wells Fargo and Regions Bank, I've specialized in maintaining and modernizing ETL pipelines. My current work involves architecting and building robust ETL solutions for machine learning models using Python, Spark, and Snowflake.

My journey in tech began in Seoul, South Korea, where my fascination was initiated by the Apple iPhone 3GS. At a time when its import was restricted, my curiosity led me to acquire one, sparking a deep-seated passion for software and its power to connect the world. This early experience set me on the path to computer science and eventually brought me to Charlotte, North Carolina. My focus on artificial intelligence, which began during my undergraduate studies in 2022, has since become the central pillar of my career. To deepen my expertise, I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Austin.

Driven by a relentless curiosity to explore diverse fields, I am always seeking new interests and challenges. I am now eager to apply my blend of data engineering experience and advanced AI/ML knowledge to a challenging AI/ML Engineer role, particularly within the dynamic tech, music, food & beverage, or aerospace industries.

Outside of technology, I'm an avid cook, a restaurant review blogger, and I enjoy the spontaneity of improv. You might also find me singing K-pop songs. These creative outlets enhance my problem-solving skills and bring a fresh perspective to my work.
